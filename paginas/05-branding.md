**H2:** Your Business Has a Face — Let People See It

People don't hire logos. They hire people — and in a community like the Tri-Cities, they usually hire people they feel like they already know. That's what branding photography does: it introduces you before you've said a word.

When I photograph a business owner, I'm not just collecting headshots and product shots.
