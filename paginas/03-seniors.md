### Sección 2

**H2:** This Year Only Happens Once

Senior year moves fast — school, games, applications, lasts of everything. Your senior pictures are the one place where all of it slows down for an evening: who you are right now, before everything changes.

I've photographed seniors across the Tri-Cities for two decades, and here's what I've learned: the best senior pictures don't come from copying someone else's Pinterest board. They come from celebrating your personality, your style, and your story. That's what we'll capture.

### Sección 3 — Objeción principal

**H2:** "I'm Not Photogenic" — Yes, You Are

Almost every senior tells me some version of this before their session. And then there's my favorite moment of every shoot: turning the camera around and showing them the back of it. Watching a shy or unsure teenager's face light up when they see just how beautiful they are — that never gets old. Not after hundreds of sessions.

You don't need to know how to pose. That's my job. I'll guide you with simple prompts the whole way — walk here, look there, take a deep breath in through your nose and out through your mouth — and somewhere between "get closer!" and "just one more!" (it's basically never just one more), you'll forget the camera is there.

### Sección 4 — Proceso

**H2:** How Your Senior Session Works

**H3: Plan (outfits, locations, the vibe)**
Before your session, we'll talk through outfits together — what shows your style, what photographs beautifully, and what you can actually move in. Then we'll pick locations around the Tri-Cities that match your vibe: riverside, open fields, urban, or somewhere that's already yours.

**H3: Shoot (golden hour, zero awkwardness)**
We meet at golden hour and keep it moving — multiple outfits, multiple spots if we've planned them, music if you want it. Bring a friend, bring your mom, bring your dog. Whatever helps you feel like you.

**H3: Reveal (the part parents cry at)**
When your gallery is ready, you'll see yourself the way I see you. I've sat with parents seeing their senior's images for the first time — eyes filling up because their "baby" has grown up, or because their child was truly captured for who they are. It happens almost every time.

### Sección 5 — Para padres

**H2:** For Parents

I know these photos are for both of you — for the announcement, the wall, and the season you're walking through as a family. My promise to you: your senior will be guided, safe and comfortable the entire session, and the photographs will show the young adult you know — not a stiff, over-posed version of them. Many of my senior families have been with me since those seniors were little.
